About me
I received my B.S. degree in Electronics Engineering and M.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering from Istanbul Technical University (ITU), Turkey, in 2012 and 2014, respectively.
In 2021, I received my Dr.-Ing. (Ph.D.) degree from the Technical University of Berlin (TUB), Germany.
Since 2022, I have been with the Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute (HHI) in Berlin, Germany.
