I am Metin Vural

About me
I received my B.S. degree in Electronics Engineering and M.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering from Istanbul Technical University (ITU), Turkey, in 2012 and 2014, respectively.
In 2021, I received my Dr.-Ing. (Ph.D.) degree from the Technical University of Berlin (TUB), Germany.
Since 2022, I have been with the Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute (HHI) in Berlin, Germany.

 

My Research Interests

My research interests are primarily centered around the resolution and exploration of optimization problems, which lie at the heart of signal processing, information theory, communication, data science, and machine learning.

Specializing in the development and application of mathematical and computational methods, my research aims to devise innovative strategies for solving complex optimization challenges. This includes enhancing algorithmic efficiency, improving decision-making processes, and optimizing system performance across various interdisciplinary domains in a broad spectrum of applications.
